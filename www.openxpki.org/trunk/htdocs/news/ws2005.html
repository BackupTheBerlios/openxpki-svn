OpenCA Workshop 2005

About

This year the OpenCA Workshop is split into two parts, the first day (Monday, 17. Oct) addresses people who are or want to get involved in the further development of OpenCA. You should have at least some knowledge about the basic ideas behind OpenCA to enjoy the day.
The second day (Tuesday, 18. Oct) targets people who are currently planing to deploy a PKI System. There will be some talks about OpenCA basics and its features and requirements in the morning and more pratical talks about success-stories and use-cases in the afternoon. Afterwards there will be some time to talk to the developers or other conference guests.
We are looking for interesting use-cases/success-stories - if you want to present your project please contact us !

Dates, Location

  • Location is the Technische Universität München in the city-center of Munich/Germany.
  • Responsible contact: Oliver Welter / Lehrstuhl fuer Datenverarbeitung
  • Monday, 17. October: developers day, will start around 10.30.
  • Monday evening (~18.30): as last year we will do some entertainment for all that are already in town.
  • Tuesday, 18. October: end-user workshop, will start at 9.00, talks end approx. 16.30, afterwards personal talks are possible.

Proceedings

  • There is a registration fee of 25 Euro (payable cash on site) that includes coffee and snacks during the day, accomodation, lunch/dinner is at your reponsibility. Note: The fee is taken directly by the university, no money is taken by the OpenCA team!
