Organizations

IETF
NIST
This is the National Institute of Standards and Technology of the United States. They publish such important standards like DES, AES or FIPS 140 series. The most important for us is the CSRC. Please note that FIPS 140-1 was replaced by FIPS 140-2 ( mapping).
SET
This is the homepage of the industrial group which promote the SET banking standard.

Companies

Chrysalis-ITS
Chrysalis-ITS is the producer of the Luna hardware security modules. They were bought by Rainbow Technologies which were bought some times later by SafeNet. Maximum security level is FIPS 140-2 Level 3 and FIPS 140-1 Level 3.
nCipher
nCipher produces several hardware security modules and accelerators. Maximum security level is FIPS 140-2 Level 3.
